Pizza Recipe

A quick homemade pizza with a soft crust, melted cheese, and your favorite toppings. Great for snacks or dinner.

Ingredents

  • 1 pizza base or dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Vegetables or meat toppings
  • A pinch of oregano

Steps

  1. Spread tomato sauce over the pizza base.
  2. Add cheese and toppings of choice.
  3. Sprinkle oregano on top.
  4. Bake in an oven until cheese melts and crust is golden.
  5. Slice and serve hot.
