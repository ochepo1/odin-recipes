Smoothie Recipe

A refreshing and healthy fruit smoothie thatâ€™s easy to make and perfect for a quick energy boost.

Ingredents

  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup milk or yogurt
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • Ice cubes
  • Optional fruits (strawberries, mango, etc.)

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients into a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour into a glass and enjoy chilled.
